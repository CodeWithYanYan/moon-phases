🐇 Rabbit in the Moon · Story Time

"A long time ago, a kind little rabbit sacrificed its pure heart to restore the Moon's fading light. The Moon Spirit accepted the offering, and from that night onward, the shape of the rabbit graced the moon's surface — watching over Earth every night."

🌙 According to ancient folklore, the rabbit's silhouette reminds us of bravery and selflessness.
